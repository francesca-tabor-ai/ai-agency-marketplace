# AI Agency Marketplace (AIAM) Readme

AI Agency Marketplace (AIAM) is a modern, B2B-focused platform designed to connect businesses with AI agencies, freelancers, and technology partners. It enables organizations to discover, compare, and collaborate with AI experts, while giving agencies and independent professionals a place to showcase their capabilities and win high-quality work.

* Make AI services discoverable and transparent
* Enable trust through clear profiles, case studies, and outcomes
